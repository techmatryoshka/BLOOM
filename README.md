# 🌸 BLOOM 🌸

> *A gentle focus app built for neurodivergent minds by a neurodivergent mind.*

Bloom is a minimalist, whimsical focus timer built with spoon theory in mind. It asks you to do just **one thing** — and surrounds you with fairies and sparkles while you do it.

Designed for people with ADHD, autism, anxiety, or anyone who needs a softer, kinder way to focus.

---

## Features

- **One task at a time** — no overwhelming lists, just one gentle intention
- **Cottagecore aesthetic** — floating fairies, sparkles, and soft colours to calm the senses
- **3 timer lengths** — 25, 15, or 5 minutes to match your energy
- **Task emoji detection** — auto-picks a matching emoji based on what you type
- **Celebration screen** — a random fairy blessing when you finish (because why not)!
- **Pause & resume** — because life happens
- **Session counter** — gentle acknowledgement of how much you've done

## How to Run

No build tools, no dependencies. Just open it!

```bash
# Option 1 — open directly in your browser
open index.html

# Option 2 — serve locally (recommended for development)
npx serve .
# or
python3 -m http.server 3000
```

## Why "Bloom"?

People with ADHD and autism often struggle with task initiation and overwhelm. Most productivity apps make this worse with long lists and complex dashboards.

Bloom asks one question: *what are you tending to right now?* That's it. The rest is just gentle encouragement.
